About Me

Name: DIVINITAS

"Divinitas" is a Latin word that translates to "divinity" or "divine nature" in English. It refers to the state or quality of being divine, often associated with God. The term encompasses the attributes and essence of a deity, including aspects like omnipotence, omniscience, and benevolence. In classical literature and theology, "divinitas" is used to describe the divine essence or godhood of deities and sometimes the divine nature attributed to exceptional individuals or things.
